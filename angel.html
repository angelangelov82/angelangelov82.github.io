My Story

May 1, 2018

I am an amateur fly angler, and I am a member of D.T.A.A for about 5 years. I don't have any preferences about fly fishing. I like to fly fish for any type of fish species. Well every fly angler has a favorite. My one is trout!

Fishing for trout could be very tricky most of the time because of her food diet. Trout is very fussy when comes to feeding. Every angler must understand the waters they fish and the flies species living around the waters. Also is essential to observe the waters for any fly hatching of. Even understanding fully the species of flies and their hatching, sometimes the weather conditions will affect the trout.

But fly fishing is not only about catching fish it is mostly about going away of the busy stressing city life. It is about doing outdoor activities which you can't do in the normal city life. I often go fishing with my friends Doncho,George and Shane and some times we do not catch any fish, but we are having great time together walking around the fields, river banks and the sea beach. For me it is electric, indescribable, fulfilled with magical views, small breeze and silence. These moments of queit and relaxing time can contribute to gaining tranquility and peace!
